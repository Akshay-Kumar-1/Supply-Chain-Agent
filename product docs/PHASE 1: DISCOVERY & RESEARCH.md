## 1\. Market Research Report

### Industry Overview

  * **Global Market Size:** \~$28.9B (2025), projected $50.2B by 2030 (CAGR: \~11.7%).
  * **The SMB Gap:** SMBs represent \~60% of businesses but only \~15% of SCM spend.
  * **The Excel Moat:** 78% of SMBs still use spreadsheets as their primary tool.

### Key Trends

1.  **AI Agent Paradigm Shift:** Moving from "chatbots" to autonomous agents that take action.
2.  **Composable Supply Chain:** Modular, interchangeable components vs. monolithic suites.
3.  **Democratization of Data:** Conversational querying (ChatGPT-style) is now an expectation.
4.  **Resilience as Priority:** Risk management is now a strategic board-level imperative.

## 2\. Competitor Analysis

### Competitive Matrix

  * **Tier 1 (Indirect):** SAP IBP, Oracle SCM Cloud, Kinaxis. (High cost, long implementation).
  * **Tier 2 (Direct):** Inventoro, Cin7, Zoho Inventory, Netstock. (Affordable but lacks deep AI intelligence).
  * **Tier 3 (Adjacent):** Generic AI (ChatGPT/Claude), Zapier/Make.com. (Flexible but manual/unstructured).

### Our Competitive Advantages

  * **Pre-built Domain Prompts:** Months of supply chain expertise encoded in the LLM.
  * **Multi-agent Orchestration:** 7 agents coordinating rather than 7 separate tools.
  * **Zero-Infrastructure:** Works directly from Excel/CSV files.
  * **Persona-Based:** Tailored outputs for Managers vs. VPs vs. Logistics.

## 6\. Jobs To Be Done (JTBD) Framework

**Core Job:** *"When I am managing my company's supply chain, I want to anticipate and resolve problems before they impact customers or costs."*

### Key Job Steps

1.  **Define:** Forecast demand and detect anomalies.
2.  **Plan:** Calculate optimal stock and replenishment.
3.  **Execute:** Create POs and monitor transit.
4.  **Monitor:** Track supplier performance and external risk.
5.  **Communicate:** Alert stakeholders and report to leadership.
